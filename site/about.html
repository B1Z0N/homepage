This derives that I am, to be honest, honest in my thoughts and in my feelings. It is kind of hard for me to lie.

I don't have any idols. Furthermore, I am freethinker. Or inidividualist, or even maverick. Giving few definitions, trying to express my thoughts precisely.

Looking forward to new experience, both positive and negative.
